Scotland's Original coffee roaster

Based in Glasgow, Thomsons has been producing tea and coffee since 1841.

Today we continue to follow the traditional methods that have pleased generations of Thomsons' customers over 170 years to offer a full range of trade, retail, coffee & tea supplies.

We supply coffee to individuals online and at the Thomsons Roastery in Thornliebank, as well as hotels, restaurants, cafes, golf clubs and many more establishments throughout Scotland and the UK.

We also have a full Private Label Service and can produce custom Tea & Coffee products for your business, just contact us for more information

Trade customer?

Get in touch. Machines, beans, pods and teas - we can provide everything both you and your customer needs.
